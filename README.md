# DSA Deck

The smart way to prepare for technical interviews. Focus on what matters most.

## What is DSA Deck?

DSA Deck helps you prepare efficiently for technical interviews by showing you which problems matter most. Instead of grinding through thousands of LeetCode problems randomly, see which problems are asked by multiple top companies and prioritize accordingly.

**The Problem:** With 3000+ LeetCode problems, how do you know what to practice?

**The Solution:** DSA Deck curates 765 unique problems from 8 company-specific lists (Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Salesforce, Neetcode 150, Blind 75, Striver 75, and Striver SDE Sheet). Problems are automatically sorted by how many companies ask them, so you can focus on high-impact problems first.

## Why DSA Deck?

- **765 unique problems** curated from 8 trusted sources (not random problems)
- **Smart prioritization** — Problems appearing in multiple company lists are shown first
- **Save 100+ hours** — No need to manually cross-reference multiple lists
- **378 problems appear in 2+ lists** — These are your highest priority
- **Company-specific prep** — Filter by company when you have a specific interview coming up
- **Track your progress** — Never lose track of what you've completed

## Features

- **Smart Sorting** — Problems sorted by number of company tags (most important first)
- **8 Curated Lists** — Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Salesforce, Neetcode 150, Blind 75, Striver 75, Striver SDE
- **765 Unique Problems** — No duplicates, just the problems that matter
- **Track by Company** — Filter by specific company when you have an interview coming up
- **Mark Progress** — Check off completed problems and track completion percentage
- **Star Important Problems** — Flag problems that need more practice or revision
- **Filter & Search** — Filter by difficulty (Easy/Medium/Hard) or search by problem name
- **Automatic Save** — All progress saved locally in your browser, no account needed

## How to Use

**For Maximum Efficiency:**
1. **Start with "All Questions" tab** — Problems are sorted by tag count (how many companies ask them)
2. **Focus on high-tag problems first** — These appear in multiple company lists and give best ROI
3. **Check off as you solve** — Click the checkbox when you complete a problem
4. **Star problems for revision** — Click the star (⭐) to mark problems that need more practice
5. **Filter by company** — When you have a specific interview, switch to that company's tab
6. **Use difficulty filters** — Focus on Easy, Medium, or Hard based on your current level
7. **Search when needed** — Find specific problems quickly with the search box

**Study Strategy:**
- **Beginner?** Start with Easy problems that have 3+ tags
- **Intermediate?** Focus on Medium problems with 2+ tags  
- **Advanced?** Tackle Hard problems and company-specific ones
- **Interview in 2 weeks?** Filter by that company + focus on problems you haven't solved

## Your Data

All your progress is saved locally in your browser. No account needed, no data sent anywhere. Your progress persists between sessions automatically.

## Access

Open `index.html` in any browser to start tracking. Works completely offline once loaded.
